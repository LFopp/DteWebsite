What is Littering in the first place?
Are waste products that have not been discarded properly other than in a container provided for that purpose.
Litter endangers our environment, our wildlife, and our economy. It pollutes our neighborhoods, decreases property values, and destroys our environment's natural beauty.



How bad is Littering?



Improperly discarded rubbish is a breeding ground for bacteria and diseases. Litter can spread diseases, viruses and parasites through two methods, direct and indirect contact. Germs can be transmitted directly by physically coming into contact with litter.
